Paidin is a peer to peer currency exchange platform, it conneects people who wants to exchange currencies, users are free to set their exchange rate and the amount they seek to exchange.

How it works: Brows the home-page for active forex post, click on the "Trade" link to start communicating. You can post your own currency exchange by registering first. Login post your currency exchange by filling this form: Select the currency you have from the "Exchange" drop-down and the currency you want to exchange from the "for" drop-down menu. Input the amount you want to exchange and your exchange rate. The converted amount will be shown. Check your desired payment options and add additional note if need be.
Interested users will reach you via the comment under the post, your will get email notification when their is an update on your foreign exchange.

More feature like auto-bargain, wallet, escrow, auto-trade and many more are coming soon.
Please always do your due deligence before exchanging with users.
